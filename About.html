About Director

Mr. Saurabh Kumar is the founder of Genius Classes in Patna. He possesses the experience of teaching in his field more than 8 years. He is a beacon of knowledge & fortitude, very popular amongst his students.

Director's Message

Dear Students & Parents,

Welcome to Genius Classes, where our inception stems from a profound commitment to delivering exceptional education. Our journey began with a vision to provide unparalleled learning opportunities for students preparing for various competitive exams.

I am pleased to share that at Genius Classes, we have successfully served students across India, helping them achieve their academic aspirations. Our relentless pursuit of excellence drives us to continually enhance our offerings and ensure student success.

At Genius Classes, we prioritize students' welfare, offering expert guidance, meticulously designed courses, and top-quality study materials. Our comprehensive approach includes targeted preparation strategies, facilitated by a team of experienced educators dedicated to empowering students to excel.

We take pride in our unique approach to mentoring, recognizing the individuality of each student. Our commitment to excellence is unwavering, and we continuously strive to provide innovative methods to maximize student potential and foster academic success. At Genius Classes, we welcome every student as part of our family, and I assure you of a rewarding learning experience guided by our dedicated team and comprehensive academic support.
